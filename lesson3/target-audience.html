Target Audience

The target audience for a business that sells laptops and smartphones can be quite broad, as these products are used by people of all ages and professions. In general, the target audience for this business is likely to be people who are in need of a new device and are willing to invest in a quality product that meets their needs. They may be looking for the latest features and technology, and may be willing to pay a premium price for a high-performing device.
Personas
  • Persona 1: "Business Professional" Age: 35-55 Gender: Male Occupation: CEO, CFO, COO, or other high-level corporate executives Income: $150,000+ Education: College degree or higher Needs: Durable, high-performance laptops and smartphones for use in a professional setting, with features such as long battery life, fast processors, and secure data storage. Ways of accessing the site: using smartphones with wifi and cellular networks They will have access to the site by personal reference in a chamber of commerce meeting.
  • Persona 2: "Tech-Savvy Student" Age: 18-25 Gender: Male or Female Occupation: Full-time student Income: $25,000 - $50,000 Education: College or graduate student Needs: Affordable laptops and smartphones with the latest features and technology, such as high-resolution displays, fast processors, and ample storage for media and academic files. Ways of accessing the site: using smartphones, laptops, and pcs. Also, wifi from restaurants, coffee shops, and cellular networks. They will have access to the site by watching Facebook, Instagram, and Youtube advertising.
    • Scenarios
  • A student is in need of a new laptop for their college classes. They are looking for an affordable option with high-resolution display, fast processor, and ample storage space for media and academic files.
  • A graphic designer is looking for a new laptop with high-performance graphics card and large storage capacity to support their design software and large files.
  • A professional traveler is in need of a durable and lightweight laptop with long battery life for use on the road. They also need a secure smartphone with a large storage capacity for their business contacts and emails.
  • A parent is looking for a durable and affordable laptop for their child to use for school and homework. They also need a smartphone with parental controls to ensure their child's safety while online.
  • An entrepreneur is looking for a laptop with a fast processor, high-resolution display, and secure data storage for their startup business and a smartphone with a large storage capacity for their business contacts and emails
  • A retiree is looking for a simple and easy-to-use laptop for internet browsing, email and video calling with family, and a smartphone with a large storage capacity for their photos.
  • A gamer is looking for a high-performance laptop with a fast processor, high-resolution display, and dedicated graphics card to support their gaming needs.
